License and pricing
===================

.. toctree::

    free-mode
    premium-mode
    pricing

|PhaserEditor|_ is a commercial product. It has a `Free <free-mode.html>`_ and `Premium <premium-mode.html>`_ mode.

By default, the IDE runs in `Free`_ mode. To enable the `Premium`_ mode you have to `unlock the IDE with a license key <premium-mode.html>`_.

You can purchase a license key in the `Gumroad Phaser Editor page <https://gumroad.com/l/phasereditor/>`_.
